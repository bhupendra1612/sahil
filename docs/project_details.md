### About the Company

Established in the year **2005** as a **Sole Proprietor Firm** at **Faridabad (Haryana, India)**, "Sahil Packaging Industries" is a leading manufacturer of all types of Pouch Packing Machines. Products are acknowledged for their **optimum strength**, **durability**, **easy handling**, and **rust resistance**. Under the leadership of **Mr. Parveen Kumar**, the company has carved a niche in the packaging industry domain.

The company offers a complete solution for all pouch packing needs, ensuring **efficiency**, **reliability**, and **customization** tailored to industry requirements.

### Key Highlights

- Aim: Offer qualitative products at affordable prices to accelerate customer's product and profit
- All machinery is fabricated using high-quality material (**SS-304/316**)
- Products can be customized as per customer requirements
- Post-sales services and ensured on-time delivery
- One of the fastest growing companies in the packaging machinery domain
